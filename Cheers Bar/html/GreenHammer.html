Green Hammer

Preperation

Preperation

  1. Pour all ingredients into a shaker with ice and shake.
  2. Then strain into a Huricaine-Glass with fresh ice.

Ingredients

Vodka 6cl
Triple-Sec 2cl
Blue Caracao 1cl
Orangejuice 4cl
Passionfruitjuice 6cl
Coca Cola
